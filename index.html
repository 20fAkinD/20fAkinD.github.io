IMAP and More

Overview

IMAP Internet Message Protocol is one of worlds well known web-technology or protocols supporting millions of users on a global scale. IT is the modern standard for mail management across multiple devices in real-time. IMAP keeps messages on the server-allowing seamless access, syncrhonization, and management from anywhere. Built for mobility and scalability and supports major provides such as Google, Yahoo, and Outlook.
